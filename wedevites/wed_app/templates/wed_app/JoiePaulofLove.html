OUR STORY


What will you do if he asked you the most awaited question?

Can I be the mother of his dragons? Obviously was a big fan of Game of Thrones. We are in this beautiful Island in celebration of our 11th year anniversary, Nungwi is our last destination on Zanzibar Island, East Africa with a fascinating view, mysterious, romantic coastlines and blissful sunsets. The ocean colors changed all day long, with such an impressive array of blues and greens sparkling away. It was strange, mesmerizing and spectacular all at the same time. The sun was radiant and danced across the ocean as the light breeze playfully whipped my hair into tangles. We’re setting our camera to capture the most awaited sunsets. I walked along the shoreline while waters and waves seem appealing with my toes in the sand and waves splashing around it. I wasn’t expecting a ring...really, but everyone had been expecting it. Everything seems to be perfect and then here we go. My racing heart was extremely aware of what was about to happen, this about to change my world and relationship status forever. He was down on one knee while the sun also kneeling for me with his hand is the most beautiful ring looks extra sparkly. A lot of wonderful things are said, I'm sure he said that “if can I be the mother of his dragons?” but I don't remember most of the things he said and it has been truly magical and of course I said YES!

When it all started?

It’s been 13 years since we first met. Take note we were only 13 and 14 years old back then. Little did I know this will be the start of our life being in each other’s arm.

We met in High School, same class together, common friends. (Fast forward) It all started with just a tease by our friends and then when our friend invites us to go and celebrates her birthday, finally he got the nerve up to ask me to be his girlfriend at 3 pm on October 1, 2005, I said YES. Our relationship/friendship started to grow too much, much more. We spent hours on the phone with one another every night and into the early morning. Even though we were young back then and even now I am positive this time in our lives contributed the both of us especially our lack of knowledge and understanding regarding all things.

From then on we were inseparable. Our story continues to college, we attended the same university then he took BS Electronic Engineering and I took BS Civil Engineering. We finish college together, faced challenges together, find a decent job and we decided to go abroad to look for a better future.

Our story may not be the same as those of others, but the concept of true love remains the same with anybody. Some might say were boring because we are just as it is, but I think it’s our private moment and we don’t really need to show anything just to prove what we are but were really beyond HAPPY. Some may ask “is this even possible to have a long-term relationship?” YES, it is!!

And this is it.
